Project Overview
This Digital Literacy Project is designed to develop practical skills required for effective participation in the digital world. It focuses on building awareness, technical competence, and responsible behavior while using online platforms.
The project includes creating an infographic to communicate digital concepts visually, developing a professional portfolio using platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and ResearchGate, and solving coding problems to understand collaborative platforms. It also emphasizes structured digital communication through formal emails and responsible social media usage.
Additionally, the project explores cybercrime awareness by analyzing real-world scenarios such as UPI fraud and identifying preventive measures. Through these tasks, the project integrates creativity, technical skills, and critical thinking, helping in building a strong and professional digital presence.
 
Task Summary
Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Created an infographic using Canva covering safe internet practices, email etiquette, and professional online presence.
Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Created and updated profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and ResearchGate to build a professional digital identity.
Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Solved a beginner-level problem on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz to understand data collection and collaboration.
Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Communication
Drafted professional emails and created a social media Do’s and Don’ts checklist to understand proper digital communication.
Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Studied UPI fraud through a case study and created a prevention checklist to promote safe online practices.
